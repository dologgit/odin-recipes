Steak

A second ago that was Medium rare steak

This recipe is so classic as it can get. Loved by millions, can be cooked by almost anyone. Truly a legend.

Ingredients:
  • Thick cut beef stake
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Salt,pepper
Steps to cook:
  1. Unfreeze steak to room temperature
  2. Remove excess moisture from steak
  3. Heat the pan and throw butter on it
  4. Start searing the steak 3 minutes each side
  5. Throw crushed garlic and thyme in searing butter
  6. Remove pan from heat when all sides was seared
  7. Put the pan in stove and turn on medium heat
  8. After 20 minutes your steak is done!
