Power rice

You broke, but tired of eating plain rice?

Try to cook this new enhanced version!

Ingredients:

  • Long grain white rice
  • 1 Carrot
  • Spinach
  • Red and yellow bell peppers

Steps to cook:

  1. Wash rice until the water is clear
  2. Pour 500ml of water in deep pan and place in on the heater
  3. Slice all vegetables as you wish
  4. Put everything in pan and wait until it boils
  5. Lower the heat to medium, and boil for 10-15 more minutes
  6. Turn off the heat and start crushing everything with potato masher
